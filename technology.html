Sensor-fusion detection & tracking

We fuse low-cost visual, thermal, and acoustic sensors into precise 3D tracks; each modality usable alone, but combined they stay robust through fog, rain, and night

Tracking models supporting asynchronous sensors, multi-target association, 3D trajectory estimation, temporary occlusions, missed detections, and handover between camera coverage zones

Engagement orchestration

We orchestrate a coordinated group of interceptors, continuously optimizing their positioning to constrain the threat's path and maneuver one drone into the final intercept

Low-altitude flight autonomy

Drones navigate autonomously below 5m, using onboard AI and sensors for situational awareness, target search, lock-on, and terminal intercept amid ground clutter and obstacles
