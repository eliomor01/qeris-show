Sensor-fusion detection & tracking

We fuse low-cost visual, thermal, and acoustic sensors into precise 3D tracks; each modality usable alone, but combined they stay robust through fog, rain, and night

Tracking models supporting asynchronous sensors, multi-target association, 3D trajectory estimation, temporary occlusions, missed detections, and handover between camera coverage zones

How it works

One Detection Node

5 EO/IR Cameras
1 Acoustic Sensor
PoE+ Switch POWER + AGGREGATION
Edge Compute Unit CPU + GPU + Qeris AI/Fusion Software
Alerting Gateway HIGH-CONFIDENCE OUTPUT

Sensor suite composed of EO cameras providing visible-light coverage, thermal cameras supporting night and low-visibility detection, and 1 acoustic sensor adding directional sound cueing. Provides 360° coverage 24/7

The PoE+ switch powers the IP cameras, aggregates their live streams, and forwards the feeds to the edge compute unit

The edge unit ingests all sensor inputs, decodes the video streams, runs Qeris AI on the CPU/GPU, and fuses the cues into a single detection event

The gateway receives high-confidence detection events and routes them to dashboards, alarms, APIs, VMS platforms, messaging tools, or remote command posts
