Prototype

Autonomous and full-chain solution for point defense, last 1-2km

  1. Detection & tracking: Detect incoming RF-silent sUAVs with low-cost sensor fusion across EO/IR and acoustic sensors below US$ 5k, with coverage up to 1.5 km
  2. Launch: Low-cost interceptors, US$ 1.5-3k, launched upon threat detection from ground launchpads
  3. Engagement orchestration: Central control continuously forecasts threats' likely paths, optimizes defenders' placement and engagement roles to contain inbound threats, and adapts approach to straight-line attacks or evasive trajectories
  4. Autonomous pursuit: Defenders autonomously navigate, track, and intercept assigned targets using AI
